Car Insurance Claim Analysis

Data Analytics Project

  1. Home
  2. Car Insurance Claim Analysis

Project Objective

The aim of this project is to analyze customer behavior and claim patterns in a car insurance dataset, in order to derive actionable insights that can help Senior Management optimize the current claims process. The final output is an interactive, logical Tableau Storyboard that flows from customer profile analysis to in-depth claim trends and ends with clear recommendations.

Steps Followed

  • Performed data cleaning and anomaly correction (e.g., fixed negative car age values).
  • Created multiple calculated fields, parameters, and filters to enable interactive exploration.
  • Built dashboards highlighting claims distribution, customer profiles, and risk segments.
  • Designed a Storyboard in Tableau that presents a logical narrative, from overview to insights and strategy.

Storyboard Flow

  1. Overview & Objective
  2. Customer Demographics and Behavior
  3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
  4. Claims Analysis and High-Risk Profiling
  5. Interactive Views with Filters & Parameters
  6. Final Business Insights & Recommendations
