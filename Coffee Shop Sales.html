Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

Data Analytics Project

  1. Home
  2. Coffee Shop Sales

Project Objective

This Coffee Sales Dashboard analyzes sales data for a coffee shop using Excel. The goal is to uncover insights that can improve business performance by examining customer buying patterns, peak sales times, and store activity.

By cleaning and structuring the data, we leverage Excel to identify trends such as:

  • Peak sales hours
  • Best-selling products
  • Revenue variations across days, months, and locations

Problem Statement

Analyze sales records and build an interactive Excel Dashboard for a coffee shop. Identify trends, patterns, and insights to enhance strategic decision-making and improve sales performance.

Key objectives include:

  • Exploring product performance
  • Identifying peak sales periods
  • Understanding revenue trends

Recommended Analysis

  • How do sales vary by day and hour?
  • Are there peak sales times?
  • Total sales revenue per month?
  • Sales trends across different store locations?
  • What is the average price per order?
  • Best-selling products (by quantity & revenue)?
  • Sales trends by product category & size?

Data Understanding

Dataset: Transaction records from Maven Roasters, a fictitious coffee shop with three locations in NYC.

  • File Type: Excel
  • Records: 149,116
  • Fields: 11
  • Time Span: Jan 2023 ‚Äì Jun 2023 (6 months)

Data Structure

Field Description
transaction_date Date of transaction
transaction_time Timestamp of purchase
store_location Store where purchase happened
product_detail Product name & size
category Product category (Coffee, Tea, etc.)
unit_price Price per unit
transaction_qty Quantity purchased
total_bill Total transaction amount

Data Preparation

Data Cleaning & Transformation (Power Query Editor):

  • Reviewed each column for errors & inconsistencies
  • Standardized product sizes (Sm ‚Üí Small, Rg ‚Üí Regular, Lg ‚Üí Large)
  • Removed leading & trailing spaces from all columns
  • Transformed transaction_time column to HH:MM:SS format
  • Created Total Bill column: unit_price √ó transaction_qty
  • Extracted Day of the Week, Month, and Hour from timestamps

Pivot Tables & Dashboard Creation

Pivot Tables & Charts were used to summarize and visualize key trends in Excel:

  • Line charts for sales trends across the day
  • Horizontal bar charts for daily sales performance
  • Pie & donut charts for product category & size contribution
  • Column charts for store footfall & revenue

Dashboard Overview

An interactive and engaging dashboard was built to help stakeholders explore:

  • Peak sales hours & busiest days
  • Top-selling products & categories
  • Revenue distribution across stores

Key Insights

Busiest Days & Times
  • Peak hours: 8:00 AM ‚Äì 10:00 AM (morning rush)
  • Highest sales days: Mondays & Fridays
Product Insights
  • Large-sized drinks dominate sales
  • Best-selling products:
    • 1st: Barista Espresso
    • 2nd: Brewed Chai Tea
Sales Across Store Locations
  • Foot traffic is evenly distributed across all stores
  • Sales opportunities remain consistent across locations
Category Contribution
  • Coffee & Teacontribute 67% of total sales
  • Coffee alone accounts for 39% of revenue
